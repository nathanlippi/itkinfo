Media Monitoring/Daily News Briefs:

 

ITK works with your communications department to develop and maintain a daily news brief ready to distribute to your internal staff.   Customized to suit your exact needs, these briefs usually cover any mentions of your company, specified competitors and general industry news from both traditional and social media. 

 

Independent PR Consultants:

 

You don’t need to work at a big agency to be able to offer your clients all of the benefits of one.  By working with ITK – you’ll walk into meetings with prospective clients fully briefed on their recent history in the press and be able to articulate your own direction for the company based on solid analysis.

 

Once they become your clients you can bring in a trusted partner to gather all the information that will show the reach of your efforts – through daily media monitoring or coverage reports on individual press releases.   

 

In addition ITK will put together briefing books on journalists so that executives walk into meetings armed with the most relevant information.

 

Consulting:

 

Though we love being the backbone of your daily research, we recognize that some companies will want to maintain their media monitoring in house.   We’ve learned a thing or two about setting up effective systems to do so and would gladly work with your in house team to make sure you’re running as efficiently as possible and that nothing’s slipping through the cracks.
